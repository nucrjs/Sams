Competition Density

Why Households per Car Wash Is the Right Metric

The Problem with Raw Counts

A market with 20 car washes could be underserved or completely saturated — it depends entirely on the household base. Households per Car Wash (HH/CW) normalizes for market size and reflects actual demand-to-supply ratio within each Sam's Xpress drive-time trade area.

Saturated
< 4,000 HH/CW
Too many washes for the household base. Membership cannibalization risk is high. Defend existing members rather than invest in growth.
Competitive
4,000–6,000 HH/CW
Normal competitive market. Performance depends on site quality and brand strength. Most of Sam's Xpress portfolio sits here.
Healthy
6,000–8,000 HH/CW
Well-positioned for growth. Demand exceeds supply — membership conversion rates are strong and new locations can be supported.
Underserved
> 8,000 HH/CW
Clear opportunity. Market is underpenetrated — early-mover advantage is available before competitors recognize the gap.
